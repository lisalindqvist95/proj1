Crashing Cords

Crashing Cords is a British indie rock band formed in 2009 by members Cassie Patterson (vocals, songwriter) and Casey Graves (vocals, lead guitar) who met as students doing their high school days in London. In 2010 the band acquired members Warren Klein (bass) and Hamzah Riggs (drums). They had a break between 2016-2017 but reunited again in 2018.

Crashing Cords is associated with alternative rock but their music is a fusion between punk rock and pop melodies.

The band have released five studio albums and two EP’s. Their latest album Cemetery Of The Living, released in 2022, received a nomination for Best Rock/Alternative Act in the BRIT Awards.
